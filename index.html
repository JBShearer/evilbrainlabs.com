Our Philosophy

Bad AI is Best AI.
Almost Good is Perfect.

Think about it: fake products cost less than real ones. They're like beta-beta products. If people like them, you build the regular beta. Then alpha. Then you're out a whole lot of money. You almost don't want the good AI—not with that curve.

Some AI is better than no AI. Bad AI is certainly there, but without the headache of being great. We're the third-worst AI company by design. We build tools that are almost good. And that's exactly the point.
